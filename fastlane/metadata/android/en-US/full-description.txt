OpenWineMer is an offline Android application for managing your entire wine cellar with a clean, modern Material You interface.
You can add, edit, sort, and organize wines using a powerful multi‑page form covering all aspects: general information, origin, grape varieties, technical characteristics, vinification, tasting notes, food pairings, commercial details, and more.

The app includes fast stock editing, dynamic sorting on any field, and full database export to JSON (encrypted or not), CSV or Excel.
All data stays on your device thanks to a local Room database.
   -> for now, the app never accesses internet. Later, maybe it will take information with the SKU from vivino.
